### **Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console**
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/)
2. Add property: `https://towpoint.in`
3. Verify ownership (via DNS or HTML file)
4. Submit sitemap: `https://towpoint.in/sitemap.xml`
5. Request indexing for main pages

### **Step 2: Google My Business (Critical for Local SEO)**
1. Create/Claim your [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/)
2. Add all locations (Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, Rajkot, etc.)
3. Add:
   - Business hours: 24/7
   - Phone: +91-94263-69900
   - Services: List all towing services
   - Photos: Add tow trucks, team photos
   - Service areas: Mark all Gujarat cities
4. Encourage customers to leave reviews

### **Step 3: Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools**
1. Visit [Bing Webmaster Tools](https://www.bing.com/webmasters/)
2. Add and verify your site
3. Submit sitemap

### **Step 4: Build Local Citations**
Register on these platforms (NAP - Name, Address, Phone must be consistent):
- **JustDial**: Add your business listing
- **Sulekha**: Local business directory
- **IndiaMART**: B2B directory
- **Yellow Pages India**: Business directory
- **Times of India Business**: Local listings
- **City-specific directories** for each Gujarat city

### **Step 5: Create Location-Specific Landing Pages**
Create dedicated pages for each major city:
- `/locations/ahmedabad` - "Car Towing Service in Ahmedabad"
- `/locations/surat` - "24x7 Towing Service in Surat"
- `/locations/vadodara` - "Emergency Roadside Assistance Vadodara"
- `/locations/rajkot` - "Tow Truck Service in Rajkot"

Each page should have:
- City-specific content (300+ words)
- Local landmarks/areas covered
- City-specific phone number (if available)
- Testimonials from that city
- Map showing service areas
